About Me

Ever since I can really remember, I have always been a creative person. From the fake magazine I would make with my friends as a kid to teaching myself how to use Photoshop at 11 years old. I was also never really in to the same things as other teenagers my age - I was into science fiction and fantasy shows and a massive Marvel movie fanatic. I loved Harry Potter and making up stories in my head about 'what if that had happened?' on a daily basis, which encouraged me to write.

That passion for the creative inspired me to be who I am today. Today, I am a 28 year-old woman who strives to be creative in many ways just because I can. My go to ways are:

  • Writing - fiction books, fan fiction
  • Graphic design - line art over pictures, random quotes, I love it all
  • Web Design - a hobby turned into so much more
  • Gamne Design - my newest hobby that I've started learning. 3D Landscapes and 2D pixel games are my favorite.

Besides that, I am an avid reader and a teacher by day. I strive to tell all my students from grades 5 to 12 that no matter what anybody says, to always be yourself and not to be afraid to like the 'unconventional'.
